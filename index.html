Introduction

I enjoy working in passionate teams that figure innovative solutions by pushing the limits of technology. I have led teams, and worked extensively to deliver complex software and silicon solutions in data processing, infrastructure and communications markets. With more than 37 issued patents, I have deep technical background in multi-disciplinary areas of software development, system level applications, architecture and hardware design with an enthusiasm to delve into new exciting areas. Currently at Microsoft, I am leading software development for Azure Big Data Software. Previosly at Xilinx, I led new application areas for data center. I focus on software technology and heterogeneous platforms to deliver breakthrough performance in the focus areas of big data, analytics, databases and machine learning. With the deep knowledge of both CPU and FPGA, I work closely with hyperscale customers for efficient deployments. Prior to this, I co-founded silicon-valley startup Velogix offering programmable logic products for compute market, which continued at IDT targeted for communications. I also led micro-architecture for network integration for Intel Xeon-D server processors. I started at Xilinx in the areas of software design.
